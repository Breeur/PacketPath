About PacketPath

PacketPath is in the business of enabling engaging experiences that simplify day-to-day activities of the human race, using IOT Devices.

IOT

We offer IoT Products, Product Design and Development services on all IoT Platforms. We have a market recognition for the products we have developed using Cypress based WiFi/Bluetooth Modules that either use Linux or WICED.

Embedded Systems

To ensure our IoT devices give you long-lasting and consistent engagements, we ensured our devices are robust and time-tested with our expertise in Embedded Systems.

Big Data

We know IoTs generate lots of data. Analysis of this data helps us develop products that better suit user needs and we use Big Data to get this Analysis done.
Our Big Data Analysis has brought us requests from organizations using IoTs, seeking our experts’ help in identifying patterns in the chaos of data their devices generate.
